My 3 Favorite Films of All Time

Terminator 2: Judgment Day

Sci Fi classic full of great action, charm, and time travel paradoxes for everyone!

Ghostbusters

Lightning in a bottle when 4 comedians at their peak get together as schlubby ghost exerminators in this bizarre 80's classic

The Godfather

Arguably the greatest film of all time, this period piece about the mafia in New York in the 1940s is a masterpiece of filmmaking and should be required viewing for anyone with a pulse.
